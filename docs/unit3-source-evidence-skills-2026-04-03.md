## Extracted Rationale

### `U3-S1` variable meaning and expression writing

- The current extraction records direct tasks on writing expressions for
  averages, prices, salt concentration, call charges, pattern rules, and counts
  with variables.
- That is enough to keep `U3-S1` focused on naming a quantity and writing a
  matching relationship, not on later symbolic simplification alone.

### `U3-S2` structure reading

- The current extraction records direct questions about coefficients, constant
  terms, term counts, like terms, and whether an expression is a monomial,
  polynomial, or linear expression.
- That is enough to keep `U3-S2` centered on reading structural roles inside an
  expression before transforming it.

### `U3-S3` substitution with structure preserved

- The current extraction records substitution tasks with signed numbers,
  fractions, powers, reciprocals, and value comparison after plugging values
  into expressions.
- That is enough to keep `U3-S3` centered on preserving sign, grouping, and
  operation meaning during evaluation.

### `U3-S4` simplification of variable expressions

- The current extraction records removing multiplication and division symbols,
  handling parentheses, using distributive structure, and combining like terms.
- That is enough to keep `U3-S4` centered on safe structural simplification of
  linear expressions with variables.

### `U3-S5` contextual modeling and cleanup

- The current extraction records geometry, ratio, pattern, and word-problem
  situations where the learner must build an expression and then simplify or
  use it.
- That is enough to keep `U3-S5` centered on context-to-expression translation
  followed by conservative cleanup.
