## Observed Problem Clusters

From the extracted pages reviewed in this session, the sources include:

- omitting `×`, `÷`, and repeated-letter notation to rewrite symbolic
  expressions
- expressing average, concentration, rate, and patterned growth situations with
  variables
- evaluating expressions by substituting integers, fractions, and signed values
- reading coefficient, constant term, number of terms, like terms, and whether
  an expression is a monomial, polynomial, or linear expression
- simplifying expressions with parentheses, distributive structure, and like-term
  combination
- rewriting geometric, ratio, and word-problem situations into algebraic
  expressions before simplifying
- reasoning about the sign or size of substituted values such as powers,
  reciprocals, and inequalities involving negative numbers
